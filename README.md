## ğŸ—ï¸ Key Structures

### 1. The Level-Based System
Every 4 months, the "Active Batch" concludes. 
* **Graduation:** Kids from **Seeds of Faith** move to **Level 2**.
* **New Intake:** A new cohort of children joins **Level 1**.
* **Scaling:** Existing students continue their journey up to **Level 5**.

### 2. City Head Camp (Islamabad)
A strategic 3-day camp designed for city leadership.
* **Objective:** Enhance decision-making and scale via park-based programs.
* **Duration:** 4-hour group sessions for city reps.
* **Output:** Actionable frameworks for local city operations.

### 3. Park-Based Level Programs
To increase accessibility, Atfal utilizes local parks for sessions, focusing on:
* Interactive group meetings.
* Team-based training.
* Practical execution of Islamic values in a public space.
